Welcome to OneWorld Public International School

One World Public International School believes in holistic development of a student by providing a safe haven amidst the surreal beauty of the Nilgiris.The school stresses on the development of cognitive, affective and psychomotor domain of a student. Our School attaches great importance to activities outside class room.Kinesthetic learning is as essential in classroom as doing helps gain better understanding. The school is appreciated for its sound moral, intellectual and physical education imparted to the students. The curriculum is designed to stimulate intellectual growth.

Our School Philosophy

We, at OneWorld Public International School, provide a stimulating, safe and supportive environment, in which all our students can aim for success. It is our mission to impart information, skills and processes which prepare students to function effectively in a rapidly changing world.
